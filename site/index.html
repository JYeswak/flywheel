Flywheel

A public engine for turning AI-assisted software work into visible loops, doctor checks, receipts, and next actions.

Business owners do not need to study every agent tool to know whether the work is disciplined. Flywheel shows the operating surface: what ran, what passed, what stayed blocked, and what got smarter for the next project.
