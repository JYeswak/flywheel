Your business already has the data.

It is just trapped in tools that do not talk: email, CRM, calendars, invoices, documents, and the manual steps between them.

I help SMB owners buy their time back. Flywheel is the public version of how I bring AI into that mess without turning the business into a lab: map the work, pick one bounded workflow slice, prove what changed, and keep the lesson for the next build.

Lessons Compound

What Owners Are Right To Worry About

AI can make a mess

That is why the first move is a workflow map, not a bot. I need to see how the work moves before I wire anything into it.

AI can sound right and be wrong

If a claim is not proven, it stays blocked. The work stays inspectable, so confidence comes from evidence instead of polish.

You should not need to become an AI expert

The Yuzu Method

The Yuzu Method is how I bring AI into an operating business without turning the business into a lab. I peel one useful workflow slice at a time, prove it in the open, keep private work private, and turn every lesson into reusable operating knowledge.

Private work stays private

Public proof is redacted, consented, or replaced with Flywheel-on-Flywheel evidence before it becomes part of the story.

Blocked is better than bluffing

If a claim is not proven, it stays blocked. The method treats visible uncertainty as a safety feature.

You approve the slice

You do not need to become an AI expert. You need a clear next workflow, a risk boundary, and proof you can inspect.
