Respect the caution

What Owners Are Right To Worry About

The page should not shame owners for being careful. It should make their caution useful by showing the controls that prevent AI from turning five disconnected systems into six.

Mess riskAI can make a mess.

That is why the method starts with mapping, not automation. The first slice is small on purpose.

Accuracy riskAI can sound confident and be wrong.

If a claim is not proven, it stays blocked. The work stays inspectable instead of relying on trust-me copy.

Change riskAI changes too fast.

New tools go through a controlled loop before they become part of the operating method.

Privacy riskClient details do not become public proof.

Examples are fully redacted, explicitly consented, or replaced with Flywheel-on-Flywheel evidence.
