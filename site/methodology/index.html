The Yuzu Method

AI adoption without operational chaos.

I bring fast-moving AI coding systems into real business workflows through a controlled loop: map the work, peel one bounded workflow slice, verify the result, and keep the lesson.

Private Work Stays Private

Before a lesson becomes reusable, Flywheel separates client-specific details from the pattern that can safely help the next project.

One Workable Slice First

The first automation target is scoped around a real manual step, a visible result, and a review path before broader workflow changes are made.

Proof Gets Deeper On Demand

Business owners see the operating method first. Technical reviewers can inspect the evidence trail behind each public claim.

What Owners Are Right To Worry About

AI can make a mess

That is why the method starts with mapping, not automation. The first slice is small on purpose.

AI can sound confident and be wrong

If a claim is not proven, it stays blocked. The work stays inspectable instead of relying on trust-me copy.

AI changes too fast

New tools go through a controlled loop before they become part of the operating method.

The Compounding Loop

MoveOwner meaningProof discipline
MapName the systems, manual steps, exceptions, and decisions.No automation before the workflow is understood.
