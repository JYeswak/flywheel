What it refuses to do

Flywheel does not ask you to trust a black box.

Every public-facing claim needs a state. Proven means tested. Blocked means not ready. Skipped means intentionally out of scope with a reason. Private means the lesson can be reused, but the customer detail stays out.

ProvenLocal or live evidence exists.

The claim can point at a run, receipt, check, or reviewed artifact.

BlockedThe missing proof stays visible.

No amount of polish upgrades a red claim.

SkippedOut of scope is named.

A skip has a reason, not a shrug.

PrivateClient detail stays out.

The lesson can move forward without exposing the work behind it.

ReusableThe next project starts smarter.

Runbooks, checks, and skills preserve what was learned.
