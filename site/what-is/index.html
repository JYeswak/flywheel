What Is Flywheel?

Flywheel is the loop engine and publication surface around disciplined agentic software work.

It owns installability, loop state, doctor checks, receipts, runbooks, and support-tier language. SkillOS is a capability control plane integration point. Proof products show the method in real contexts without making those products the mission ceiling.

Loop engine

Turns agent work into ticks, validated closeouts, and next actions.

Boundary keeper

Separates public engine claims from private fleet state and product-specific proof surfaces.

Evidence habit

Records what is proven, what is only a compatibility target, and what remains blocked.
