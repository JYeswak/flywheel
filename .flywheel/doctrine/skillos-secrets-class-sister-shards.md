## Boundary

SkillOS owns the canonical capability-control-plane doctrine. Flywheel owns the
fleet-facing sister shards, AGENTS indexes, and public/operator explanation of
why these rules affect repo safety.

This doc intentionally points to SkillOS locators instead of mirroring their
full text. The ownership gate from `flywheel-bmbub` exists because cross-repo
propagation without an ownership class is itself a failure mode.
