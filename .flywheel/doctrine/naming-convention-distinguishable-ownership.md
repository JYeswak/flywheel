## Anti-patterns

1. **Premature renaming sweep before Wave-0 ships** — would entangle the
   rename pass with substrate-stability work; tested wrong. Wait for Wave-0.

2. **Picking a convention without plan-arc** — naming is plan-space work
   (per `feedback_three_reasoning_spaces.md`). Mistake cost in plan-space is
   cheapest; in code-space ~25× plan.

3. **Folding Peel/Press/Pour as the WHOLE convention** — those are ONE
   verb-set inside the Yuzu Method. Other bands (detection, governance,
   recovery, observation) need their own Yuzu-coded vocabularies.

4. **Treating distinguishability as approachability**
   ("generic names are easier for newcomers") — Joshua's mission isn't
   onboarding strangers, it's building a self-sustaining company.
   Distinguishability is the constraint.
