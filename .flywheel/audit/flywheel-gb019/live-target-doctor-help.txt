usage:
  flywheel-readme draft <artifact-path> [--out PATH] [--from-bead ID] [--drafted-by NAME] [--pane SESSION:PANE] [--dry-run] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme submit <readme-path> [--bead ID] [--drafted-by NAME] [--pane SESSION:PANE] [--dry-run] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme review --queue [--limit N] [--older-than-hours N] [--json]
  flywheel-readme review <readme-path> [--reviewed-by NAME] [--non-interactive] [--dry-run] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme reject <readme-path> --reasons CODES [--detail-file PATH] [--reviewed-by NAME] [--dry-run] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme pass <readme-path> --reviewed-by NAME [--dry-run] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme signoff --queue [--limit N] [--older-than-hours N] [--json]
  flywheel-readme signoff <readme-path> [--signed-by NAME] [--reject-with-reason TEXT] [--dry-run] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme doctor [--scope queue|ledger|locks|frontmatter|validation_commands] [--fix] [--apply] [--json]
  flywheel-readme health [--watch] [-i SECONDS] [--json]
  flywheel-readme repair --scope queue|ledger|locks|frontmatter|validation_commands [--dry-run] [--apply] [--explain] [--idempotency-key KEY] [--json]
  flywheel-readme validate <readme-path> [--gate 1|2] [--json]
  flywheel-readme audit [--limit N] [--json]
  flywheel-readme why <readme-path|audit-id> [--json]
  flywheel-readme --info [--json]
  flywheel-readme --examples | examples [--json]
  flywheel-readme quickstart [--json]
  flywheel-readme help <topic> [--json]
  flywheel-readme completion bash|zsh|fish
  flywheel-readme schema <command> [--json]
  flywheel-readme metrics|logs|trace|palette|activity|triage [--json]

Universal flags: --json --no-color --no-emoji --width N
Mutation flags: --dry-run --explain --idempotency-key KEY
Exit codes: 0 success, 1 domain failure, 2 usage, 3 transient, 4 blocked, 5 lock, 6 frontmatter, 7 ledger, 8 validation.
